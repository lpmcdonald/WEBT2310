About Us

Since opening its doors in 2010, Midnight Blue Salon & Spa has quickly built an excellent reputation for providing clients with a haven for both body and spirit. Located in the scenic city of Chattanooga Tennesee. The professionals at Midnight Blue Salon & Spa are committed to the well-being of our clients and specialize in providing every client with a uniquely tailored experience. Our approach to health and beauty is subtle yet strong.​​​​​​

Enter our salon and spa, and enjoy a wealth of services tailored for your individual needs. Experience dedication and professionalism when you receive a treatment from one of our talented artists. At Midnight Blue Salon & Spa, your well being is our highest priority.

Midnight Blue Salon & Spa is proud to be the FIRST Salon and Day Spa in Tennessee to receive the Tennessee Performance Excellence Award for management and customer service. Our staff has spent hundreds of hours in training, so that we may offer our clients the best Chattanooga hair salon and spa experience possible.
