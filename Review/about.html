TRIPLICITY

Triplicity is a family harmony group hailing from south Georgia. They’ve opened up for legendary acts of all genres from Bon Jovi to Alabama. The three are signed with Hector Management & Happy Highway Touring; and are managed by singer-songwriter and producer, Alan Shelton .

Kelsey, Kendall and Kyle Pearson are triplets from Bainbridge, Georgia. They began singing together in church and realized their harmonies were something special. In addition to the classic hymns, the trio also have secular influences in their music such as rock bands Journey, Queen and Foreigner, along with quintessential country legends like Loretta Lynn and Johnny Cash. The three keep their lyrics honest and their music full of roots and authenticity. They are currently recording a studio album, produced by Alan Shelton and are continuing to write for future projects. Performing all over the country in venues like arenas, theaters, and outdoor music festivals, the three have a love and passion for traveling together and meeting their fans. Kelsey, Kendall and Kyle take great pride in writing their own music and connecting with fans through their lyrics. Together, the three encompass what it means to be proud southerners with a love for what they do. Triplicity is bringing a new “old school country with a rhinestone flair” to Nashville.

Triplicity has always believed in the power of their fans. They are leading examples of what it looks like to follow your heart and to do what inspires you. Fans and promoters not only love the music, but always have a complimentary word to say about the amazing energy and enthusiasm that the three bring to the stage. Kelsey, Kendall and Kyle are three performers who represent what hard work, perseverance, and passion look like. Together, they are TRIPLICITY!
